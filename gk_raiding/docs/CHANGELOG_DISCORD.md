## Stellaris 4.4.4

### Plundering

- Bombardment plundering now copies the loot instead of stealing it: the raider gains double the take, the target keeps their resources, and only the raider is notified.
- Invasion plunder raised to 18 months of output.

### Despoliation Traditions

- Void Reaver pirate-waystation buff now adds a flat +1.5% collection, the same as a Reaver Stronghold.

### Compatibility

- Updated for Stellaris 4.4.4.
