## Stellaris 4.4.1 "Pegasus" / Nomads Update

### Plundering

- Resource raiding uses the vanilla Plundering bombardment stance, opened to every plunder-capable empire. Each tick siphons a share of the planet's current output.
- Invasion plunders about a year of a conquered colony's output.
- Plunder-war surrender seizes half the loser's physical resource stockpiles plus a share of their specimens, with a notification.
- **Steal Relic** war goal (new): on surrender, steals one random relic and a share of specimens. Available to any plundering empire.

### Despoliation traditions

- **Raider's Fervor** (reworked): +50% orbital bombardment army damage, and Unity for each enemy ship and army destroyed, scaled by size.
- **Forward Raiding Bases** (replaces Predatory Bombardment): non-nomads unlock a starbase building that skims 3% of nearby non-ally colonies within 3 systems each tick. Nomads instead build the three pirate waystation modules. Void Reavers get +50% pirate-waystation collection.
- **Restless Raiders** (replaces Triumph in Ruin): +10 stability and 15% reduced amenity usage after a recent victory, +5 stability while at or recently at war, and 5 lost stability after 20 years of peace.

### Raiding civics

- Every empire type now has a pop-raiding and a resource-raiding civic.
- **Reavers** (Standard): native plundering, Plunderer commander trait, 5% reduced ship upkeep, +1 enclave capacity with Overlord.
- **Corsair Protocol** (Machine): native plundering, Shipbreaker commander trait, 5% reduced ship upkeep, +5% weapon damage, +10% chance to steal defeated ships.
- **Ravager Swarm** (Hive): pop and resource raider. +100% purge workforce, +10% food jobs.

### Pirate waystations

- Letters of Marque and the hive/machine resource-plunder civics can build the three pirate waystation modules when nomadic.

### Arkships

- Nomad empires with the Boarding Cables technology can build Boarding Cables on an arkship (one per arkship, fills three auxiliary slots), giving a chance to steal defeated ships and capture enemy commanders.

### Other

- Heirs of the Khan can use the raiding kit.
- Plunderer added to the Acquisitive Officers trait pool.
- Emancipation raiding no longer causes an opinion penalty.
- Declaring a Raiding, Emancipation, or Reaping war auto-authorizes raiding the target.

### Removed

- Predatory Bombardment and Triumph in Ruin traditions, replaced by the reworked tree.
- The Hive Bodysnatcher hack. Vanilla Bodysnatcher reverts to stock behaviour.
- Custom Great Khan content (Khan flagship override, Marauder Doomstar titan) and the Satrapy specialist subject. Vanilla provides its own.
- The post-war "Ransacked" colony aftermath.

### Compatibility

- Updated for Stellaris 4.4.1.
