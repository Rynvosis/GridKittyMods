Added the full species trait suite: 57 traits (37 positive, 20 negative) across nine files in common/traits/ split by gate and polarity - global, common, phenotype, and cyber/biogenesis, plus special. Shared gate blocks live in common/inline_scripts/traits/ (portrait lists per overlay category, genemod access per polarity, species_class_override per gate). CYBER-leg traits reach MACHINE and ROBOT archetypes via the is_archetype branch from trait_pc_ark_preference, and reach ascended cyborgs regardless of portrait via a species_class_override on has_trait = trait_cybernetic. Phenotype and biogenesis legs carry the Mutation-ascension override so gene modding can apply them across phenotypes. Replaces the Tinkerer placeholder (now trait_gk_tinkerers) and the three marker traits, whose portrait lists became the inline gate scripts.
Added gk_diet_upkeep_mult static modifier in common/static_modifiers/: one key fanning out to the three organics upkeep modifiers with a single consolidated tooltip, used by five traits. Added common/script_values/gk_script_values.txt with counter_gk_cannibalistic (clone of counter_familial, planet-scoped, capped at 20000) and gk_aeroplankton_size_mult.
Last Children scales -0.5% workforce per 100 own-trait pops empire-wide and uncapped, against a flat +30%, netting roughly +5% at game start. Pop counts are raw, so per-100 rates encode as the rate divided by 100 - the same encoding trait_familial uses; Cannibalistic's -0.1%/100 follows the same form.
