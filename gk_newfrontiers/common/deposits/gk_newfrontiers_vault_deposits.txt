## Sterile World district-capacity deposits: energy, mineral and vault families.
## Sterile is rich in energy and minerals both (mineral-saturated crust, exposed
## geothermal), so it draws high on each, plus a vault family matching Volcanic's
## polytechnic distribution in 15_infernals_deposits.txt.
##
## One set serves two masters, exactly like the d_volcanic_* deposits: the wild
## galaxy generator picks by drop_weight, and gk_sterile_world_setup composes a
## capital total from the same pool via while + random_list. Bigger deposits are
## rarer in the wild (+2 = @high, +3 = @low), following vanilla.

@high = 16
@med = 8
@low = 4

# ##########
# Energy family (favours the +2 while-pool, then +1 and +3 fixed picks)
# ##########
